About Us Services

D5 Design & Build is an architectural and construction company that specializes in creating innovative and modern architectural designs and constructing them from foundation to completion.


The company, formerly known as "CEFERINO B. DORIA JR., Architects", was founded in 2010 by Architect Ceferino B. Doria Jr. Since its inception, D5 Design & Build has completed numerous projects ranging from residential houses to commercial buildings.


Architect Ceferino B. Doria Jr. plans to take the company to greater heights by expanding it in 2024. As part of the expansion plans, the company was rebranded to "D5 Design & Build".

CEFERINO B. DORIA JR., Architects offers a wide service, from designing from scratch to building completion. The company is equipped with professional architects who give out the best and modern architectural designs with the direct supervision of Architect Ceferino B. Doria Jr.

The Company is also a general contractor and offers construction services. From architectural designs, the company also creates all the various plans needed for the building. Providing construction materials, managing construction projects, overseeing workers and subcontractors, overseeing safety on-site, overseeing quality control for construction works, and handling any necessary permits or licenses are services also offered by the company.

In addition, the company offers consultation services for clients who want professional advice on their current or future projects and other architectural or construction queries or problems.
